Asylum Applications from Afghanistan in 2021

Partnership between the United Nations and Parsons School of Design for Data Visualization Major Studio 1
Created by Tatiana Kalainoff

103 million people are forcibly displaced worldwide (as of mid-2022 based on UNHCR estimates).

This visualizaiton shows how applications from their country of origin to their desired country of asylum to their final outcomes. Here we are just seeing applications coming from Afghanistan which make up for approximately 2.8 million refugees.

For those forcilbly displaced from their home countries, this can be used as a tool to gain insight into which countries may be more likely to accept their application for asylum.
